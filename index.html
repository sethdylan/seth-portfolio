Why did you decide to become a web developer?

I wanted to focus all my energy into a career path that is challening, exciting, and rewarding. Web development encompases all of this and more. When I am coding I seem to learn something new everyday. I love the feeling of creating something new and also solving complex problems while coding.

When did you start coding?

I started about a year and a half ago and I have been coding daily ever since.

I am interested in coding, where does a newbie start?

I started with free online coding courses such as Code Academy and Khan Academy. I would recommend starting small, with free online courses and see if you enjoy it. If you really like what you are doing, look into more intesive online courses such as CareerFoundry, SKilcrush, General Assembly.
